About Me

Hey there, I'm Sanjay Rajbhandari, and I'm all about bringing creativity, aesthetics, and functionality into the digital world. With a passion for innovation and a knack for turning ideas into visually stunning and efficient solutions, I'm on a journey where graphics designing, front-end web development, and software development through Visual Basic converge.

With a paintbrush in one hand and a mouse in the other, I've been crafting visual masterpieces through time periods. My experience as a graphics designer has honed my eye for detail and my ability to transform concepts into captivating visuals. Whether it's designing eye-catching logos, striking posters, or immersive user interfaces, I'm up for the challenge.

I'm an intermediate-level web developer who thrives on crafting captivating digital experiences. With a focus on responsive, user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I ensure they look great and work flawlessly across devices. Beyond front-end design, I delve into software development through Visual Basic, creating practical solutions for real-world problems, from desktop applications to databases.

What sets me apart is my ability to balance the creative with the logical. I bring the best of both worlds to every project. I'm the artist who codes and the coder who designs, ensuring that your projects are not only visually captivating but also technically robust.
